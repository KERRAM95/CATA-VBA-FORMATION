SimAnimationTypes (Enumeration)

enum SimAnimationTypes {
  SimUndefAnimation,
  SimScaleFactor,
  SimTimeHistory,
  SimLoopOverModes,
  SimScanSingleFrame,
  SimTimeHistoryAndLoopover,
  SimFrameBased,
  SimStreamline,
  SimEventSeries,
  SimHarmonic
}
The animation types.
Values:
SimUndefAnimation
Undefined animation.
SimScaleFactor
Divides the complete range in specified divisions.
SimTimeHistory
Animates over the frames of all time-based steps.
SimLoopOverModes
Animates over the modes of all frequency/harmonic steps.
SimScanSingleFrame
Animates over value from actual min to actual max.
SimTimeHistoryAndLoopover
Its a mixed animation. Animates over all the frames reguardless of step type (History and ModeLoop).
SimFrameBased
History Animation over over multiple datasets.
SimStreamline
Only animate streamlines. Deformation and results are constant.
SimEventSeries
Only animate event series. Deformation and results are constant.
SimHarmonic
Similar to scaled animation but for harmonic deformation and results.
