SimConfiguration (Enumeration)

enum SimConfiguration {
  SimVPFourSquare,
  SimVPTriLeft,
  SimVPTriBottom,
  SimVPTwoRow,
  SimVPTwoColumn,
  SimVPSingle
}
The configuration information for multi views.
Values:
SimVPFourSquare
To divide viewer into 4 equal viewpoints in 2x2 fashion.
SimVPTriLeft
To divide viewer into 4 unequal viewpoints with 3 viewpoints in first column and another 1 in second column.
SimVPTriBottom
To divide viewer into 4 unequal viewpoints with 3 viewpoints at the bottom row and another 1 on top of them.
SimVPTwoRow
To divide viewer into 2 equal viewpoints in two rows.
SimVPTwoColumn
To divide viewer into 2 equal viewpoints in two columns.
SimVPSingle
To return to the default single viewpoint mode.
