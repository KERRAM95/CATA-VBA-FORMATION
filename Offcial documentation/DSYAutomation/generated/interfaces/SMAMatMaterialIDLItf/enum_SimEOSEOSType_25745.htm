SimEOSEOSType (Enumeration)

enum SimEOSEOSType {
  SimEOSIdealGas,
  SimEOSJWL,
  SimEOSUsUp,
  SimEOSTabular
}
Type of EOS.
Values:
SimEOSIdealGas
Material with an ideal gas equation of state.
SimEOSJWL
Material with an explosive equation of state.
SimEOSUsUp
Material with a user-defined equation of state that is defined in user subroutine VUEOS.
SimEOSTabular
Material with a tabulated equation of state that is linear in energy.
