SimLowDensityFoamStrainRateType (Enumeration)

enum SimLowDensityFoamStrainRateType {
  SimLowDensityFoamVolumetric,
  SimLowDensityFoamPrincipal,
  SimLowDensityFoamMaxPrincipal
}
The strain rate type measures when Poisson effects are neglected.
Values:
SimLowDensityFoamVolumetric
The volumetric strain rate.
SimLowDensityFoamPrincipal
The strain rate along each principal direction to evaluate principal stress in that direction.
SimLowDensityFoamMaxPrincipal
The maximum of principal direction strain rates to evaluate stress-strain response along each principal direction.
