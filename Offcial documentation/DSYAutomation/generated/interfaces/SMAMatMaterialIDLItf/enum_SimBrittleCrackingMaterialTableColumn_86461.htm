SimBrittleCrackingMaterialTableColumn (Enumeration)

enum SimBrittleCrackingMaterialTableColumn {
  SimBrittleCrackingDirectStressAfterCracking,
  SimBrittleCrackingDirectCrackingStrain,
  SimBrittleCrackingDirectCrackingDisplacement,
  SimBrittleCrackingFailureStress,
  SimBrittleCrackingModeIFractureEnergy,
  SimBrittleCrackingTemperature
}
Columns in material table.

The columns in the material table will be:

For SimBrittleCrackingStrain of brittle cracking
Values:
SimBrittleCrackingDirectStressAfterCracking
Direct stress after cracking. Quantity: STRESS, Units: N_m2
SimBrittleCrackingDirectCrackingStrain
Direct cracking strain. Quantity: DIMENSIONLESS, Units: None
SimBrittleCrackingTemperature
Temperature (optional). Quantity: TEMPRTRE, Units: Kdeg

For SimBrittleCrackingDisplacement of brittle cracking
SimBrittleCrackingDirectStressAfterCracking
Direct stress after cracking. Quantity: STRESS, Units: N_m2
SimBrittleCrackingDirectCrackingDisplacement
Direct cracking displacement. Quantity: LENGTH, Units: m
SimBrittleCrackingTemperature
Temperature (optional). Quantity: TEMPRTRE, Units: Kdeg

For SimBrittleCrackingGFI of brittle cracking
SimBrittleCrackingFailureStress
Failure stress. Quantity: STRESS, Units: N_m2
SimBrittleCrackingModeIFractureEnergy
Mode I fracture energy. Quantity: ENERGY_AREA, Units: J_m2
SimBrittleCrackingTemperature
Temperature (optional). Quantity: TEMPRTRE, Units: Kdeg
