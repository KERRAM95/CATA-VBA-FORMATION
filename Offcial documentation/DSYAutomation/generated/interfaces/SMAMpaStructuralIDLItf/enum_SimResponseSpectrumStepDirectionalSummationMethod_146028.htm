SimResponseSpectrumStepDirectionalSummationMethod (Enumeration)

enum SimResponseSpectrumStepDirectionalSummationMethod {
  SimResponseSpectrumStepAlgebraic,
  SimResponseSpectrumStepSquareRootOfSumOfSquares,
  SimResponseSpectrumStepFortyPercentRule,
  SimResponseSpectrumStepThirtyPercentRule
}
Method for combining the multidirectional excitations depending on the nature of the excitations.
Values:
SimResponseSpectrumStepAlgebraic
Sums the peak responses in the different spatial directions for each mode.
SimResponseSpectrumStepSquareRootOfSumOfSquares
Sums the total peak response in each excitation direction by taking the square root of the sum of the squares of those responses.
SimResponseSpectrumStepFortyPercentRule
Sums the response for all possible combinations of the three components, assuming that when the maximum response from one component occurs, the response from the other two components is 40% of their maximum value.
SimResponseSpectrumStepThirtyPercentRule
Sums the response for all possible combinations of the three components, assuming that when the maximum response from one component occurs, the response from the other two components is 30% of their maximum value.
