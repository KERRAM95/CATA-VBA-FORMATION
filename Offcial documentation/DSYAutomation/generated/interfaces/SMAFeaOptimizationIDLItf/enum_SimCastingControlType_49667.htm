SimCastingControlType (Enumeration)

enum SimCastingControlType {
  SimSingleDraw,
  SimSplitDraw,
  SimAutoDraw
}
The casting control type.
Values:
SimSingleDraw
Indicates that all draft angles and features are in one direction (the casting is contained in one section of the mold).
SimSplitDraw
Indicates that the mold is split, so the directions of the draft angles and features depend on which half of the mold they are in.
SimAutoDraw
Indicates that the casting process is not established.
