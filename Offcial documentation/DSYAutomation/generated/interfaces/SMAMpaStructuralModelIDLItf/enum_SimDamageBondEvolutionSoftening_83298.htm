SimDamageBondEvolutionSoftening (Enumeration)

enum SimDamageBondEvolutionSoftening {
  SimDamageBondLINEAR,
  SimDamageBondEXPONENTIAL,
  SimDamageBondTABULAR
}
The evolution softening type.
Values:
SimDamageBondLINEAR
Specify a linear softening stress-strain response (after the initiation of damage) for linear elastic materials or a linear evolution of the damage variable with deformation (after the initiation of damage) for elastic-plastic materials.
SimDamageBondEXPONENTIAL
Specify an exponential softening stress-strain response (after the initiation of damage) for linear elastic materials or an exponential evolution of the damage variable with deformation (after the initiation of damage) for elastic-plastic materials.
SimDamageBondTABULAR
Specify the evolution of the damage variable with deformation (after the initiation of damage) in tabular form. This option can be used only in conjunction with TYPE=DISPLACEMENT.
