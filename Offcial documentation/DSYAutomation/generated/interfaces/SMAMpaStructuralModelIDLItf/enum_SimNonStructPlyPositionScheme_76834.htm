SimNonStructPlyPositionScheme (Enumeration)

enum SimNonStructPlyPositionScheme {
  SimNonStructPlyPositionUnDefined,
  SimNonStructPlyPositionTopOnly,
  SimNonStructPlyPositionBottomOnly,
  SimNonStructPlyPositionTopAndBottom
}
The method used to offset the composite section.
Values:
SimNonStructPlyPositionUnDefined
The dummy ply position is not defined.
SimNonStructPlyPositionTopOnly
Dummy ply is to be positioned at the top of stacking and has thickness which is the sum of thickness of all non structural plies.
SimNonStructPlyPositionBottomOnly
Dummy ply is to be positioned at the bottom of stacking and has thickness which is the sum of thickness of all non structural plies.
SimNonStructPlyPositionTopAndBottom
One dummy ply is to be positioned at the top of stacking and one at bottom of stacking each having thickness which is the 0.5 * (sum of thickness of all non structural plies).
