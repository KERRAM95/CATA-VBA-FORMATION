SimEulerianVolumeFractionType (Enumeration)

enum SimEulerianVolumeFractionType {
  SimVFTSpecified,
  SimVFTComputed,
  SimVFTUniform
}
Method for specifying the Volume Fraction Type used for the Eulerian Material Assignment
Values:
SimVFTUniform
Indicates that the volume fraction will be 1.0 for all the elements of the Eulerian Property support
SimVFTSpecified
Indicates that the volume fractions will be 1.0 for the elements of the Material Assignment support
SimVFTComputed
Indicates that the volume fractions will be calculated from the support
