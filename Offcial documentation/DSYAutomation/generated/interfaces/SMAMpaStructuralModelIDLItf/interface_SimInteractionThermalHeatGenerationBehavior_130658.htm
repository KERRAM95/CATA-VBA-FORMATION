SimInteractionThermalHeatGenerationBehavior (Object)

Represents Thermal Heat Generation Behavior in Interaction Property.

FractionOfEnergyToHeatForElectricalSolidConductance Retrieves or sets the fraction of energy to heat for electrical solid conductance.
FractionOfEnergyToHeatForMechanical Retrieves or sets the fraction of energy to heat for mechanical.
SpecifyForElectricalLiquidConductanceFlag Retrieves or sets the flag which determines if the electrical liquid conductance is specified.
SpecifyForElectricalSolidConductanceFlag Retrieves or sets the flag which determines if the electrical solid conductance is specified.

UserDefinedFlag Retrieves or sets the user-defined flag.
